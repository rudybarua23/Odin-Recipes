Margherita Pizza

Delicious pizza made in minutes

Very tasty pizza that can be easily made

Good taste and relatively cheap ingredients

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. Galbani Mozzarella Cheese, shredded
  • 1.5 oz. Galbani Parmesan, grated
  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp. garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp. sea salt
  • 8 plum tomatoes, sliced
  • 12 in. pizza crust
  • 10 basil leaves, shredded

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Stir together olive oil, garlic, and salt; toss with tomatoes, and marinade for 30 minutes.
  3. Brush pizza crust with some of the tomato marinade. Sprinkle the pizza evenly with mozzarella. Arrange tomatoes overtop, then sprinkle with shredded basil and parmesan
  4. Bake in preheated oven until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown, 7-10 minutes depending on the oven settings
